  The stairs wind up again. It is good to be with one's family, relaxed in  the well known chair. Not a worry in the world.    He sat there, his mind at ease, not caring much about anything. He  didn't even look up when the clock on the mantel whirred, and the  ridiculous bird popped out of its nest to herald a new day.
