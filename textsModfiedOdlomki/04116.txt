Marge kept trying to protest, but I simply grabbed her and Doreen and  hustled them out to my car. Doreen lived in a wooded, hilly section a  little north of White Plains. I made it in ten minutes.    *       *       *       *       *    Marge had said Elmer worked in the garage. We skidded to a stop with our front end hanging over what looked like a  bomb crater in the middle of the driveway.    I swallowed my heart down again, while I backed away fast.
