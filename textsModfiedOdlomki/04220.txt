"So it may be that they will continue onward of their own choice. If  they make no choice, momentum will carry them onward, perhaps forever.    "But in any case, Nanlo and Hugh Neils will have exactly what they have  asked for--each other, for all eternity. If truly that was what they  wanted, a great destiny may be theirs. A lifetime of travel can bring  them to the stars. They or their descendants can be the first humans to  bridge the gap of nothingness that has thus far daunted the stoutest  hearts."    As they watched, the green dart of light dwindled and was gone. And  quite invisible at last in the arms of outer darkness, the Vulcan sped  its two passengers onward toward the stars.
